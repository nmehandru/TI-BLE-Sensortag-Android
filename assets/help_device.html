Device View Help

When the view is started a Service Discovery is initiated. This may take up to ten seconds depending on the number of services the device offers. When Service Discovery is completed, all GATT services are listed. The details displayed are the UUID and number of characteristics. Additionally the service name will be shown, providing it is a service defined by Bluetooth Sig or is a service from TI's SensorTag or Simple BLE Peripheral applications. Click on one of the listed services to view and operate its GATT characteristics
